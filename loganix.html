Should I leave in “[company] hr” because they might have the same potential, or is that not the intent we’re looking for?

Given vague intent keywords, is it my job to filter intent without knowing who it’s for?

I can filter out clear non-aligned keywords, but for the nuance that you’re looking for, I feel there needs to be a who’s it for.

Otherwise, I’ll have my idea of who’s it for that might be completely different from what you and the client have in mind.
