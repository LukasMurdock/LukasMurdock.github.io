## Outdoor & Adventure

- Surf in Waikiki, walk to Duke Paoa Kahanamoku Statue
    - Rent boards right on the beach ($20-30)
    - Rent boards across the street (Moku, $15)
- Beaches
- Magic Island
- Makapuu Lookout Point
- Halona Blowhole
- Halona Cove
- Sandys Beach Park
- Hikes
    - [Diamond Head](https://dlnr.hawaii.gov/dsp/parks/oahu/diamond-head-state-monument/) (reservation required for non residents)
    - Koko Head
- [Byodo-in Temple](https://byodo-in.com/)
    - Ring the bell three times, light incense, see koi fish, get Omikuji.
- [Gunstock Ranch](https://gunstockranch.com/): Horseback Riding
- [Kualua Ranch](https://www.kualoa.com/): movie tours, horseback, beach.
- [GoJump Hawaii Skydiving](https://gojump-hawaii.com/eng/)
- [Waimea Valley](https://www.waimeavalley.net/) nature preserve
- Booze Cruise
- Every Friday: Waikiki [Hilton Hawaiian Village Fireworks](https://hiltonhawaiianvillage.com/events/friday-night-fireworks/)
